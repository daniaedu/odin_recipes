Avocado Toast

Description

Avocado Toast is a delicious and healthy source of fiber and fatty acids.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice sourdough bread
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 3 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Handful of arugula
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 lemon zest
  • 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon chia seeds

Steps

  1. Toast bread to desired doneness, 3 to 5 minutes.
  2. Mash avocado in a bowl; stir in lemon juice, lemon zest and sea salt
  3. Spread avocado mixture on toast
  4. Top with chia seeds, crushed red pepper flakes, halved tomatoes and arugula
